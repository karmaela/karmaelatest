Karmaela represents balance and versatility and exploring the idea of creativity…

My name is Michelle and Karmaela is my contribution to the world wide web. I am a Sydney based Graphic Designer and Visual Artist with an Honours degree in Fashion and Textiles Design from The University of Technology Sydney. In pursuit of transforming life’s pleasures from hobbies to passion, I have set up this website to share my life’s work and love for creativity.

Inspiration is limitless, and Karmaela draws inspiration from artists such as Escher, Dali and Ben Heine, also designers such as Thierry Mugler and Gareth Pugh, as well as musicians including Trent Reznor, Bob Marley, Beth Hart and Morcheeba. Other vendors for my imagination include Mr Joss Whedon, the great weapons designer Gil Hibben, pop culture and the futuristic, the fantastical and mythological, and last but not least, everyday humans I meet and mingle with from all walks of life.

 

What you will find on this website:

An array of Visual Art including; portraiture, fantasy art and  murals. From my  first mural contribution at age 9 to the endless commissioned artworks for collectibles, charities, publications, exhibitions, restaurants and individual memorabilia. Various works are available for purchase or order.

Fashion Designs such as  The Singularity Effect collection; showcased at UTS The Future Of Fashion 2013, as well as countless fashion illustration and design work available for your viewing pleasure or purchase. As the winner of Cooper St Creation in 2012, my design was realised for production. I have also designed apparel in fashion wholesale, directed and styled photo shoot and headed costume and wardrobe departments for two short films.

My career choice is as a Graphic Designer and currently I am working for Contare | Quality Sleepwear and its partner apparel wholesaler and as an in house Graphic/Apparel/Textile designer as well as a content and marketing manager, I have done work across vast arenas of graphic design including pre-press, point of sale, promotional graphics and packaging as well as work in photography, catalogue, cover art and branding. My art has also been printed in various books and collectible cards.
